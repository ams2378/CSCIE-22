Section 6

Midterm exam

The midterm exam will be held on October 19 during the first half of lecture in Harvard Hall 104 (our usual classroom).

If you are a distance student, review the distance exam policy. In short:

  • Students from the six New England states must come to campus to take the exam.
  • Students from outside New England must arrange for a proctored exam. See the exam policy for more details.

Exam details

  • You will have 60 minutes to complete the exam.
  • The exam is “open book”, that is, you may use any notes, textbooks, and the coursepack. However, you may not use a personal computer or any other computing device.
  • The questions are worth a total of 50 points. In order to properly budget your time, plan on spending approximately one minute per point.
  • The exam consists of two parts:
    • Part I has 5 multiple-choice questions, each 3 points.
    • Part II has 3 short-answer questions.

These details will be printed on the first page of the exam itself.
